On Simplicity

March 25, 2026

There’s something satisfying about stripping things down to their essentials. In software, in writing, in design — the best work often comes from removing rather than adding.

I’ve been thinking about this a lot lately as I rebuild my personal site. The temptation is always to reach for a framework, add a build step, pull in dependencies. But what if you just… didn’t?

A static site generator, some HTML, a bit of CSS. That’s really all you need. The content is what matters, and everything else should get out of the way.
